The OPUMO Connect WooCommerce Tracker sets up the tracking pixel on a Merchant's cart.

== Installation ==

1. Contact OPUMO Connect for your Merchant ID.

2. In the plugin's settings (OPUMO icon on the WordPress sidebar), enter your Merchant ID and click "Save Settings."
